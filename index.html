Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new dictionary?

Tap the "+" button on the main screen to create a new dictionary. Give it a name, choose an icon, and configure your fields.

Can I add images to my flashcards?

Yes! When creating or editing an item, tap on any image field to either take a photo or select one from your photo library.

How does the learning algorithm work?

LearnByAssociation uses a smart algorithm that shows you difficult items more frequently. Items you mark as "hard" will appear more often than items you know well.

Can I share my dictionaries?

Yes! Use the Export feature in dictionary settings to save your dictionary as a file, then share it with others who can import it into their app.

How do I remove ads?

Upgrade to LearnByAssociation Premium to remove all ads and unlock additional features. Tap on any ad to learn more about premium options.

Troubleshooting

The app crashes when I add an image

Make sure you've granted camera and photo library permissions in Settings > LearnByAssociation.

My dictionaries disappeared

Check if you're signed into the same iCloud account. If you recently restored your device, you may need to restore from a backup.

Import isn't working

Make sure the file you're importing is a valid .json file exported from LearnByAssociation. Files from other apps won't work.
