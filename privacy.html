Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Overview

LearnByAssociation ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Dictionary Content: Text and images you add to your learning dictionaries
  • Learning Progress: Your study sessions, scores, and learning statistics

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, iOS version, and unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, which features you use
  • Crash Reports: Information about app crashes to improve stability

Information from Third Parties

  • Advertising: Our advertising partners (Google AdMob) may collect information about ad interactions
  • Analytics: We may use analytics services to understand app usage

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app's functionality
  • To save your learning progress and dictionaries
  • To display relevant advertisements (for non-premium users)
  • To improve the app and fix bugs
  • To provide customer support

Data Storage

  • All your dictionary data is stored locally on your device using Core Data
  • We do not upload your personal content to any servers
  • iCloud sync (if enabled) uses Apple's secure infrastructure

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements (non-premium users)
  • StoreKit: For processing premium subscriptions

These services have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (all data is stored locally on your device)
  • Delete your data (by deleting dictionaries or the app)
  • Opt-out of ads (by purchasing a premium subscription)
  • Control permissions (camera, photo library) through iOS Settings

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is intended for general audiences.

Data Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • We implement standard iOS security practices
  • We do not transmit personal learning content over the internet

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy.
